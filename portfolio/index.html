About Me

I am currently an apprentice learning Java at the Software Guild in Louisville, KY. I have studied and worked professionally as a researcher and graduate instructor in the field of sociology for over a decade. During this time, some of my most enjoyable collaborations have been with teams developing websites for data analysis and presentation. As an apprentice at the Software Guild, I am learning skills that I hope will enable me to build tools like these.

Music and art are part of my everyday life. In my personal time, I enjoy songwriting and photography. I unwind by learning a new Chopin sonata. In the past, I have had opportunities to explore ceramics and to film and edit an educational documentary overseas.
